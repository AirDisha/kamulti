PARTICIPANT GUIDE

How to prepare for a Kundalini Activation session — and what to do afterwards

A Kundalini Activation session is a deep and beautiful process of tuning your nervous system to a high frequency. The facilitator simply creates a high-frequency field; your own energy responds to it like a tuning fork. No willpower is required from you — only relaxation and trust.

BEFORE — Nutrition & Hydration

Light food

In the 24 hours before the session, avoid heavy food and animal products (meat).

Fast before the session

Do not eat for at least 2 hours before the start. The process flows better on an empty stomach.

Water is your conductor

Drink enough clean water before and after the session.

Strict prohibition

In the 24 hours before your session, completely avoid alcohol, caffeine and any psychoactive substances.

BEFORE — Clothing & Jewellery

Comfort

Choose comfortable clothing that does not constrict the body anywhere.

White clothing

We strongly recommend light-coloured clothing, ideally all white. White symbolises purity and helps attune to higher vibrations.

No jewellery

Before the session begins, remove all metal, wood and stone jewellery.

FOR ONLINE SESSIONS

Space

Prepare a safe, quiet space where you will not be disturbed for around 1.5 hours.

Surface

Prepare a soft, flat surface (yoga mat, blanket) where you can lie at full length.

Camera

Position your camera so the facilitator can see your whole body.

DURING THE SESSION

Intention

You may set a personal intention before the start, but do not fixate on it.

Switch off the mind

Your main task is to move from logical control into total relaxation.

Don't analyse

Allow yourself to experience each moment without wondering "what's happening now?" Simply surrender to the process.

AFTER THE SESSION — Integration

Be gentle with yourself

In the first few hours your consciousness may feel "floating". Cancel tasks requiring hard logic. Give yourself rest.

Emotional waves are normal

Activation often brings suppressed emotions to the surface. A flash of irritation or deep sensitivity is an ecological cleansing process. Observe without judgement.

Sustaining your resource

For 24 hours after the session, keep drinking plenty of water. Strictly avoid alcohol and caffeine.

Remember: the session is only the beginning. Your journey of spiritual opening is just unfolding!
