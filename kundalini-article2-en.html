KRIYAS, VISIONS & THE LEFT-BRAIN FREEZE

How to decode the symptoms of awakening?

← About Kundalini

Kundalini activation is not simply a meditation that leaves you feeling mildly relaxed. It is a deep metamorphosis, sometimes accompanied by quite unusual physical and emotional manifestations. Without understanding their nature, one might be alarmed. But with knowledge of the physiology, it becomes clear: the body is doing brilliant work of self-healing.

1. Kriyas: the body's spontaneous dance

During activation, many notice involuntary movements — twitching, back arching, shaking. In yoga these are called "kriyas". Neurobiology offers a straightforward explanation: an imbalance occurs in the inhibitory neurons of the brain's motor centres. Years of accumulated, "frozen" stress and trauma energy is literally shaken out of the neural pathways. Your body is releasing its blocks.

2. Visions and "transcendent sight"

Some people see inner imagery, flashes of light, or notice that the world seems unreally vivid after a session (the "eyes of love" effect). These are not hallucinations. When the energetic process peaks, the thalamus and limbic system activate, temporarily opening gateways to imagery from the inner world.

3. The freeze effect

Sometimes instead of a surge of energy, a person feels heaviness, paralysis or pressure on the left side of the head (the "left-brain freeze"). This is in fact an ancient protective mechanism of the vagus nerve. When there is too much energy, the body temporarily "switches off" the logical left hemisphere to prevent system overload. The key rule: don't resist — breathe through the process.

4. The die-off phase

Sometimes after a vivid awakening flash, deep fatigue sets in. The immune system enters a catabolic state: macrophage cells begin "dismantling" spent structures in the organism. This is an internal "moulting": the old is broken down to free space for a new, greater capacity in your nervous system.

All these states are not the goal of the process — they are simply signs that your old "armour" is burning away. Allow your body to make this journey, and after the cleansing phase, a stage of extraordinary lightness and freedom will surely follow.
