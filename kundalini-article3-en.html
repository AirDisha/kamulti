EARTH'S KUNDALINI

Why the Age of Aquarius is the time of feminine spiritual leadership

← About Kundalini

Did you know that Kundalini is not only an energy found within the human body? Just as a hidden life force lies dormant within each of us, our planet possesses its own energetic axis. And right now, before our eyes, the Earth is undergoing its own global activation.

The movement of the Luminous Serpent

Scholar and esotericist Drunvalo Melchizedek describes Earth's Kundalini as a special planetary energy that moves like a serpent. Every 13,000 years (according to the astrological cycle of the precession of the equinoxes) this energy changes its position. When one cycle ends, the polarity of Kundalini shifts and the planet's energetic centre moves. This event alters Earth's electromagnetic grid — and in its wake, the frequency of human consciousness itself.

The end of the masculine cycle and the beginning of New Light

In 2012, the previous cycle completed. The old ways of controlling the world — based on rigid masculine dominance — began to exhaust themselves. We see this everywhere: chaos, crises, the collapse of old systems. But for those who understand the sacred meaning of what is happening, there is no cause for fear. The Earth has entered the constellation of Aquarius. The Age of the Feminine has begun.

What does this mean for each of us?

Spiritually, this is a time when the feminine principle will lead humanity toward the light — not through conquest or logical dominance, but through intuition, acceptance, softness and deep connection with the Heart.

It is planetary Kundalini that is now awakening in people a mass yearning for Truth — not only in ashrams and monasteries, but in ordinary cities, people are beginning to seek God within themselves and to work with body and energy.

If you feel an irresistible pull towards knowing yourself, if your own Kundalini energy is beginning to stir — know that you are not alone. You are resonating with a global process happening to the Earth itself.
