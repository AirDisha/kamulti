WHAT IS KUNDALINI ACTIVATION

Kundalini Activation (KAP) is an energy transmission that initiates the natural awakening of dormant kundalini energy at the base of the spine. It is not yoga or meditation in the classical sense.

This process is top-down — it flows from the Higher Self toward the personality. This makes it safe even for beginners.
